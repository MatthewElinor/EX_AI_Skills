# Card Art Prompts

AI art prompts for generating card artwork in flat cartoon style.

## Style Keywords

Always include these for consistency:
- `flat cartoon style`
- `game card art`
- `simple shapes`
- `bold colors`
- `centered composition`
- `minimal details`
- `white background`
- `clean lines`

## Aspect Ratio

- Standard: `--ar 3:4`
- Wide: `--ar 16:9`

## Attack Card Prompts

### Melee Attack

```
[WEAPON/CHARACTER] in attacking pose, flat cartoon style game card art, dynamic action, simple shapes, bold red and [SECONDARY] colors, centered composition, motion lines, minimal details, white background, game asset --ar 3:4
```

Examples:
- Strike: `warrior sword strike, flat cartoon style game card art, dynamic pose, bold red and gold, centered composition, motion blur, minimal details, white background --ar 3:4`
- Bash: `heavy mace swing, flat cartoon style game card art, powerful impact, bold red and gray, centered composition, impact sparks, minimal details, white background --ar 3:4`

### Ranged Attack


### Multi-Target Attack

## Tips for Best Results

1. Keep subject simple and recognizable
2. Use 2-3 colors maximum
3. Avoid complex backgrounds
4. Emphasize the action/effect
5. Test multiple variants per card
