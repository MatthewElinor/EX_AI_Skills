# Relic Art Prompts

## Style Keywords

Always include:
- `flat cartoon style`
- `game relic icon`
- `simple geometric shapes`
- `bold colors`
- `centered composition`
- `minimal details`
- `white background`
- `glowing effect`

## Icon Design Tips

1. Subject should fill 70-80% of frame
2. Use 2 colors maximum for clarity
3. Add subtle glow for rarity indication
4. Keep silhouette simple and recognizable
5. Test visibility at 64x64 pixels
